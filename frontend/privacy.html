Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Stay or Leave collects, how it's used, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash, never in plain text). If you upload a profile picture or photos with a review, we store those files. If you contact us, we keep the details of your message.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to operate your account, show your reviews and comparisons, respond to support requests, and send essential account emails such as password resets and email verification.

3. What we don't do

We don't sell your personal information. We don't share your email address with other users. Reviews and comments you post publicly are visible to other visitors, as you'd expect from a public review.

4. Cookies and local storage

We use browser local storage to keep you logged in and to remember your dark/light mode preference. We don't use third-party advertising trackers.

5. Data retention and deletion

You can delete your own reviews and comments at any time from your profile. To request full account deletion, contact us through the Contact page and we'll remove your account and associated personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where we're required to retain records by law.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed before storage, all authenticated requests use signed tokens, and file uploads are restricted by type and size. No system is perfectly secure, so we encourage using a unique password for your account.

7. Children's privacy

Stay or Leave is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify account holders by email.

9. Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent through our contact page.
