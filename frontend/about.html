Why we built this

Big decisions deserve better than a gut feeling

Most people choosing between two countries, two job offers, or two colleges end up relying on forum threads and secondhand opinions. We wanted one place that puts the real numbers next to the real experiences.

Our mission

Give anyone facing a "stay or leave" decision — a move, a job change, a school choice — a clear, side-by-side view of what actually changes, backed by data rather than assumptions.

Our vision

A world where no one moves country, switches jobs, or picks a college on guesswork alone — where the information that should be easy to find, finally is.

How we got here

Timeline

2023

The idea

Started as a spreadsheet comparing two job offers in different countries. Sharing it with friends made clear other people needed the same thing.

2024

First comparisons go live

Launched country-to-country comparisons across cost of living, safety, and healthcare for a handful of popular destinations.

2025

Reviews and community

Added the ability for people to share what actually happened after they made the move — the part no spreadsheet can capture.

2026

Companies and colleges

Expanded beyond countries and cities to cover company offers and college choices, using the same side-by-side approach.
